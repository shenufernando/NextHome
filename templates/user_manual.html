User Manual & Guide

Learn how to easily find, buy, rent, or sell your property on NextHome

1

Create an Account

Click on the Sign Up button at the top right corner. Fill in your name, email, and choose a secure password to register as either a Buyer or Seller.

2

Search and Filter Properties

Use the main search bar on the Home page to type locations (e.g., Colombo, Kandy). Apply filters for price brackets, property type (House, Apartment, Land), and size to narrow down choices.

3

Contacting Sellers

Once you find a perfect listing, use our **Direct Messaging system** to safely and instantly chat with the verified property owners to schedule visits or negotiate pricing.

4

Posting a Property Listing

If you are a seller, navigate to your dashboard, click "Add Property," upload high-quality images, specify pricing, and submit. Our admins will verify the control data before it goes live.
